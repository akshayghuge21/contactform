How to create forgotten password?

Step-1: Enter your register email or phone number.
Step-2: Click on send otp button.
Step-3: Enter valid otp number.
Step-4: Create your new password.
Step-5: Confirm your password. Step-6: Press Submit button and get your password on register email or phone number.
